Experimental Procedure


Create algae culture with stock and algae-grow solution, made from water and nutrient concentrate

Let the cultures grow for a few days and build the amount of cells before beginning experimentation

Measure initial cell density with hemocytometer, following the diagram below if need be

Incubate in centrifuge for 3 hours at 0, 1000, 2000, or 3000 x g (12 trials per condition), then wait 2 weeks

Take final cell density with hemocytometer, using the same method as described previously

Analyze results by calculating cell density and performing statistical tests
